## Launch Strategy: Phase 1 (Months 1-3)

**Primary**: Real estate agents (emlakçılar) in major cities who are frustrated with Sahibinden's high fees.

**Profile**:
- Small to medium-sized emlak offices (1-10 agents)
- Currently paying 2,000 TL/month to Sahibinden
- Tech-savvy enough to use online platforms
- Active on Facebook and WhatsApp groups

### Value Proposition
**"Same features, 90% cheaper"**

- Sahibinden: 2,000 TL/month = 24,000 TL/year
- Alsatbedava: 200 TL/month = 2,400 TL/year
- **Savings: 21,600 TL/year per agent**

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Agent Adoption
**Mitigation**:
- Aggressive free trial (3 months)
- Money-back guarantee
- White-glove onboarding
- Referral incentives

### Risk 2: Sahibinden Price War
**Mitigation**:
- Focus on superior UX, not just price
- Build loyal community
- Add unique features (AI recommendations, virtual tours)

### Risk 3: Technical Issues at Scale
**Mitigation**:
- Load testing before launch
- Error monitoring (Sentry)
- Database optimization
- CDN for images

### Risk 4: Legal/Regulatory
**Mitigation**:
- Consult Turkish e-commerce lawyer
- GDPR compliance
- Clear Terms of Service
- Content moderation system
